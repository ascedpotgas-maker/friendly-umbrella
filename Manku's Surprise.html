Yesterday, Today and Forevermore

Yesterday, I met you, and my heart skipped a beat,

Today, I love you, with every step, every repeat.

Forever, I'll cherish you, through laughter and through tears,

With you, my love, my heart beats clearer

.

Yesterday's moments, we shared a glance,

Today, our love's a beautiful dance.

Forever's the promise, we'll make to stay,

Together, our love will find its way

.

In your eyes, my heart finds a home,

With you, I am never alone.

Yesterday, today, and forevermore,

I'll love you, my darling, forever in store

.

You are my sunshine, my guiding light,

My love for you shines through the dark of night.

Yesterday, today, and forevermore,

My heart belongs to you, forever in store.

.

Fair Maiden

Fairest maiden of the wire's gentle might,

Whose voice, like sweetest melody, doth take flight,

In whispers soft, and tones of tender light,

Thou didst captivate my heart, a love so bright

.

In chance encounter, o'er the phone's dark stage,

Our paths didst cross, and forth a spark didst engage,

A flame that burns, a passion that doth not abate,

For thee, my love, my heart doth consecrate

.

Thy laughter, music to mine ears, doth ring,

A joyous peal, that doth my soul's deep spring,

Thy words, a gentle breeze, that doth my heart entwine,

In love's sweet snare, I am forever thine

.

Oh, fairest phone belle, with voice so divine,

How doth my heart yearn for thee, in love's sweet shrine,

To see thy face, to hold thy hand, to be,

With thee, my love, in love's sweet ecstasy

.

Yet, till that day, when fate doth bring us near,

I'll cherish every word, each tender tone, so dear,

And in my heart, thy love shall forever stay,

A flame that burns, a love that doth not stray.

.

A Blessing From God

You are a gift from above,

A blessing from God, a labor of love.

Sent to bring joy to my life,

A treasure to cherish, a precious wife

.

Your smile brightens up my day,

Your laughter, a melody that never fades away.

Your eyes, a window to your soul,

A reflection of the love that makes me whole

.

You are a blessing, a precious find,

A treasure to cherish, a love that's one of a kind.

God sent you to me, a gift so divine,

A love that's pure, a heart that's truly mine

.

With you by my side, I feel complete,

Together we navigate life's joys and defeats.

You are my partner, my friend, my guiding light,

With you, everything feels just right

.

I'll cherish and love you, with all of my heart,

Grateful for the blessing, that we never depart.

You are a gift from God, a treasure so rare,

Forever and always, my love for you will be there

.

More Than Words

More than words can say,

More than any language can convey,

The love I feel for you,

Is deep, true, and forever new

.

In your eyes, my heart finds home,

With you, I am never alone,

Your touch ignites a burning fire,

Melting fears, and soothing desire

.

More than words can express,

The way you make my soul feel blessed,

The laughter we share, the tears we've cried,

The memories we've built, side by side

.

With every breath, I'll love you more,

Through life's ups and downs, forevermore,

In your arms, I find my peace,

With you, my love, my heart releases

.

More than words can say,

My love for you will find its way,

Through every moment, every test,

Forever and always, you'll be my best
